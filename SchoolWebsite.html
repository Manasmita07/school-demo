INTRODUCTION

In this digital era, no school can claim not to have an online presence. A website forms an information and communication involvement core, easily accessible to students, parents, staff members, and the community at large. Whether you are setting up a new website or refreshing an old one, this tutorial will take you through all that you need to know when it comes to putting up your school website for increased visibility of the school and more functionality for the users.

WHAT IS SCHOOL ERP

The school website is an institutional cyber portal regarding academic programs, events, policies, and many more. It should be one avenue in showcasing the achievements the school has undertaken to promote communication and valued resources to prospective and current students, parents, and staff..

A well-designed school website will act as a digital brochure, a communications hub, and even a learning tool. It's key in ensuring that all the stakeholders have been kept abreast of relevant information, allowed to make inquiries, and gleaned an overview with ease.

TEN SIGNS YOUR SCHOOL REALLY NEEDS A
SCHOOL WEBSITE

If your school has traditionally used paper flyers or phone calls, a website offers a faster and more efficient means of communications.

If there are complaints from parents or students that they never receive schedules and details of events, then your website can serve as a real-time information source.

A website will represent your school in a better light and make it visible and more attractive to potential students.

If your office is tired of spending too much time answering routine questions, a website provides FAQs and automated responses.

A website allows integration of e-learning materials to hence support distance education.

For schools that don't allow online fee payments, a website can sort this out for you.

A website will allow hosting of parent portals and discussion forums, hence improving engagement.

If this is what is happening with your school's achievements, inform the outside world by highlighting the awards, student work, and news on the website.

A website allows you a venue to post downloadable resources for distribution to parents, and these are bound to reduce the workload of the administration.

You can relay urgent news quickly about closures or schedule changes through the website.
