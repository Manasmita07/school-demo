INTRODUCTION

The School ERP system aims at managing the school effectively with the help of the right tool. From admissions to attendance, academics, finance, communication, or even HR tasks, a School ERP provides solutions for all in one package for modern educational institutions. In this guide, one gets to know all about the installation of School ERP, its benefits, and most importantly, how to fully exploit an investment in it.

WHAT IS SCHOOL ERP

School ERP software is a system wherein all administrative and academic functions are combined into one platform. It makes the management and coordination of school activities easy. It provides automated solutions in every process, including fee collection, student attendance, management of staff, scheduling of examinations, and more.

The ERP empowers the backbone of the administrative framework by enhancing data accuracy, communication, and real-time access to information at the school for administrators, teachers, students, and parents alike.
